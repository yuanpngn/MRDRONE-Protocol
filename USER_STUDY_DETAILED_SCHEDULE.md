## **Emergency & Contingency Protocols**

### **Scenario 1: Battery Runs Out Mid-Flight**
1. CrazyFlie will automatically land (safety feature)
2. Yuan: Immediately swap to fresh battery (B5, B6, or B7 backup)
3. Rog: Apologize to participant, explain brief technical issue
4. Restart flight routine from beginning
5. Log incident: battery ID, participant, time, circumstances

### **Scenario 2: CrazyFlie Malfunction**
1. Activate emergency land
2. If drone unresponsive, use physical kill switch
3. Swap to backup CrazyFlie Brushless 2.1 unit
4. Quickly test backup unit (30-second motor test)
5. Resume participant session
6. After session: troubleshoot primary unit

### **Scenario 3: Meta Quest 3 Battery Dies**
1. Ju: Quickly plug in Meta Quest 3 to charging cable
2. Option A: Wait 5 minutes for quick charge to 10-15%, then resume
3. Option B: Skip MR.Drone condition, proceed with Drone-Only only, note in log
4. Rog: Keep participant engaged with conversation during wait

### **Scenario 4: Participant Feels Unwell (VR Sickness)**
1. IMMEDIATELY remove Meta Quest 3
2. Guide participant to seated position
3. Offer water
4. Stop drone (emergency land)
5. Wait 5-10 minutes for recovery
6. Ask if participant wants to continue or withdraw
7. If continues: proceed with Drone-Only condition only
8. Log incident thoroughly

### **Scenario 5: Propeller Breaks Mid-Flight**
1. Drone will likely land automatically (imbalanced thrust)
2. Yuan: Power off, replace broken propeller (spare props ready)
3. Test motor balance (3-second test)
4. Resume flight
5. Estimated swap time: 2-3 minutes

### **Scenario 6: Battery Fire/Smoke**
1. **EVACUATE IMMEDIATELY**
2. Do NOT use water
3. Use Class D fire extinguisher or LiPo fire bag
4. Call emergency services if fire spreads
5. Clear room until fire department arrives
6. Document incident for safety report

# INTERVIEW QUESTIONS - REFINED & EXPANDED

## **Section 1: Initial Experience (After Each Condition)**

### **Presence & Co-Location (MR.Drone Condition)**
1. **On a scale of 1-5, how "present" did the virtual drone avatar feel?** 
   - *Follow-up:* What would make you rate it higher? Lower?

2. **Did the avatar feel perfectly aligned with the physical drone, or did you notice any misalignment?**
   - *Follow-up:* If misaligned, describe what you saw (e.g., offset position, lag, rotation error).

3. **At any point, did you forget you were wearing a headset and fully immerse in the experience with the avatar as a partner?**
   - *Follow-up:* Describe that moment. What triggered the immersion?

### **Expressiveness & Perceived Agency**
4. **What specific movement or gesture made the avatar feel most "alive" or intentional to you?**
   - *Follow-up:* Was it the speed, direction, height, or something else?

5. **Did you sense any emotions or intentions from the avatar (e.g., curious, shy, playful, assertive)?**
   - *Follow-up:* What visual or motion cues communicated that emotion?

6. **If you could describe the avatar's "personality" in 3 words, what would they be?**

### **Safety & Proxemics**
7. **When the avatar/drone approached you closely, what was your immediate physical response?**
   - *Options: Did you freeze, step back, reach toward it, turn away, lean in, or something else?*
   - *Follow-up:* Why did you respond that way?

8. **Did you feel you could safely predict where it would move next?**
   - *Follow-up:* Were there any "near-miss" moments that surprised you or made you hesitant?

9. **What distance felt most comfortable for you? What distance felt too close or too far?**

### **Workload & Cognitive Demand**
10. **What aspect of the experience demanded the most mental effort?**
    - *Options: Tracking visually, timing your movements, managing personal space, understanding instructions, navigating the VR interface*

11. **Did you reach a "flow state" where you felt fully engaged without overthinking?**
    - *Follow-up:* What helped you reach flow (e.g., music, avatar motion, clear cues)? What broke it (e.g., lag, distraction, confusion)?

### **Timing, Synchronization, & Music**
12. **Did the music and the avatar's motion feel synchronized to you?**
    - *Follow-up:* If not, how off did they feel? (e.g., delayed by 1-2 seconds, completely unrelated)

13. **Did the music help you connect with the avatar, or did it feel distracting?**

14. **Were the on-screen panels (About, Instructions, Start/Stop button) visible and helpful at the right moments?**

### **Design Feedback**
15. **If we could change ONE thing about the avatar's appearance or motion for the next iteration, what should it be?**
    - *Examples: Color, size, shape, smoothness of motion, speed, trajectory patterns*

16. **What single behavior or feature would make the avatar feel more like a dance partner to you?**
    - *Examples: Responding to your movements, "eye contact," pauses, mirroring, leading/following dynamics*

### **Direct Comparisons**
